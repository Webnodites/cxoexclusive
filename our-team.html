Meet Our Team

Harsh Wardhan

(CEO) Business Consulting & Board Practice

Harsh has spearheaded Business Units and operations in large multi-national corporations including; PepsiCo, Jindal Urban Infrastructure, Securitas, G4S, Shaw Wallace, UNICEF and other large Indian companies. He has been operating as CEO for the last 25 years. Harsh has been acknowledged as the Best Security Professional in the private sector in India. As CEO of G4S he has built an organization in India with 120,000 personnel, the largest security company in the world.
Harsh has extensive board and executive management experience and an exceptional track record of achieving business goals in diverse and complex businesses. He has developed strategies for growth, created business architecture and has also developed and delivered strategic plans. He has cross-functional experience and cross industry expertise ranging from FMCG to Services and Infrastructure. He has worked with many MNC clients. numerous Embassies including American, British, EU and UN agencies. He is also credited with commissioning India's first and largest Waste-to-Energy plant, as CEO of Jindal Urban Infrastructure Limited. Harsh holds an MSc (BITS Pilani) and an MBA (FMS Delhi University).

Ashok Sakuja

Skills & Training Advisor

The General is a qualified interviewing officer from Defence Institute of Psychological Research,(DIPR), Delhi and specialises in conducting selection of candidates for positions at higher levels in the Corporate and Government assignments, through psychometric techniques.
He has also worked at senior levels in the Private Security Industries having multinational presence. He played significant roles in raising Terra Force Security from the grass roots level, which was a DLF initiative in 2007, transforming Walsons Security in to multinational company namely Securitas India Ltd in 2009-2010. He also pioneered training initiatives in Pinkerton Ltd, a multinational company involved in security training, corporate due diligence, security risk analysis and their mitigation from 2011-2013.
In 2015 the General was appointed CEO of Security Sector Skill Development Council (SSSDC) under the aegis of Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE).
An exponent of conceptualising, developing and conducting training both in the Army and in the corporate environment, the General officer has attained expertise in the fields of operations and development of Security Skills.
